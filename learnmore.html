What this website is about

This website explains how the Information Age and Artificial Intelligence (AI) shape the modern world. It introduces how technology, data, and intelligent systems have changed the way people communicate, work, learn, and live. The site gives an overview of what the Information Age is, how AI works, and how both technologies impact society—such as in education, healthcare, industries, and everyday life.
