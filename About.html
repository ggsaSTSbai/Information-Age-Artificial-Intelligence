About This Project

This website, The Information Age & Artificial Intelligence, is a project for our Science, Technology, and Society course under Instructor Miss Vienice Jonah Jee Garcia. It explores the development and impact of the Information Age, focusing on how digital technologies and AI shape modern society. The site presents well-researched content on key technological advancements, historical milestones, and the ethical, social, and technical issues associated with AI, providing a clear and engaging learning experience for users.

The project also aims to enhance critical thinking, digital literacy, and communication skills. Through interactive features, quizzes, and multimedia content, users can actively engage with the material, gaining a deeper understanding of how information technologies and AI influence daily life, decision-making, and the broader social landscape.
